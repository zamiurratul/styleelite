ABOUT US

COMPANY DESCRIPTION

Style Elite Limited is an apparel buying house to meet customer demands of all seasons and all tastes. We have professionals and experts in design, merchandising and quality assurance.

We provide reliable services of cost assessment, sample design and development, production planning and follow up, quality inspection, documentation and shipment.

Quality and timely delivery are the most important factors in the fashion industry. We are committed to our customers and stakeholders to ensure such. Commitment, integrity and accountability are the key values that we pursue to maximise our customer satisfaction.

We have fully compliant factories as sources of our manufacturing. Our values associates are

  • MN Group.
  • Radiance Knitwear Limited.
  • Dynamic Sweater Industries Ltd.
  • White Swan Limited.

Company Strategy

Vision, Mission, Strength & Values

Our Vision

  • To bring color in life through style.

Our Mission

  • Our mission is to manufacture and provide superior quality clothes at the best price with maximum customer satisfaction preserving humanity and nature.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We are honest, open, ethical, and fair.
  • Accountability: We remain accountable to our partners, stakeholders and people.
  • Diversity: We meet the demand and tastes of people across diverse cultural backgrounds.
  • Commitment: We are committed to our customers to maximize their satisfaction by ensuring quality and on-time delivery.
  • Respect for people and environment: We strive to show a deep respect for human beings, communities and environment in which they live.

Our Strength

  • Style: We have a team of creative and innovative designers.
  • Quality: We ensure highest quality of our products.
  • Production Planning and Monitoring: Our team of professionals and experts ensure flawless production planning and continuous monitoring.
  • Cost: We ensure maximum cost benefit to our valued customers.
