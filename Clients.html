MANUFACTURING SOURCES & CLIENTS

Manufacturing Sources

MN GROUP

MN Group is one of the successful manufacturers in Bangladesh with experience of over 33 years. MN Group assures the perfect blend of quality and efficiency. It manufactures both woven and knit products. The group has three production units with more than 1100 machines and production capacity of 3,50,000 pieces per month. The group employs more than 1600 workers and management personnel.

Radiance Knitwear’s Limited

Starting in 1999, Radiance Knitwear’s Limited has become a recognized knitted apparel manufacturer. Pain staking efforts taken in each step of value chain – procurement, production process and planning, workforce productivity and efficiency have become its core competencies. With 500+ machines and over 1500 workers the factory produces 400 tons of knit fabric per month and 2,00,000 pieces of various knit products per month.

Dynamic Sweater Industries Limited

Dynamic Sweater Industries Limited is one of the pioneer sweater manufacturers in Bangladesh which started its production in 1993. Integrity, sincerity and commitment are the keys to its success. It produces mainly sweaters, cardigan, pullover, scarf etc.

White Swan Limited

Imbued with the motto ‘we believe in inclusive growth’ White Swan Limited was founded in 2015. Quality, on-time delivery, diversity are its core competencies. Employing over 100 workers and management personnel and about 70+ machines the factory produces 50,000 pieces of knit products in a month.
