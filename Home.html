Fashion is a language of style

Bangladesh is one of the leading ready-made garments (RMG) manufacturing and exporting country to the global market. This RMG sector is a multi-billion dollar manufacturing and export industry with about 4.4 million workers and export volume worth US $ 28 billion. The RMG basket includes knit and woven goods including shorts, trousers, shirts, skirts, sweaters, blouses, skirts, t-shirts, jackets, sports attire and many more casual and fashion wear.

Style Elite Limited (SEL) is a proud member of the RMG sector of Bangladesh. It is an apparel buying house owned by four fashion enthusiast friends. The company started its journey on 13 June 2017.

Imbued with the motto ‘style, Comfort and Experience’, Style Elite Limited envisions becoming a leading contributor in the fashion world.
